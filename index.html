FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

Why Should I Blast My Vehicle?

Paint finishes can hide rust and previous repairs on the metal surfaces. By blasting, we can expose damaged or corrosive areas before re-painting.

What is Dustless Blasting?

Dustless Blasting is a revolutionary paint stripping and cleaning system that can remove virtually any coating from almost any surface, without creating a huge plume of dust.

What Makes Blasting Dustless?

The Dustless Blaster works by mixing water and abrasive inside the blast tank. By introducing water, we are drastically increasing mass and energy the machine is putting out, while suppressing dust.

Will it Rust After Blasting?

Vintage Iron uses a film-less rust inhibitor that allows for up to 72 hours of rust protection. We recommend having your primer lined up before we arrive, or schedule for us to have supplies ready on arrival. We encourage you to primer your vehicle as soon as possible for optimal results.

Will it hurt glass or chrome?

Glass and chrome can be damaged by any kind of abrasive blasting. You should always mask off or remove the chrome and glass prior to blasting.

Do I need a deposit?

Before our arrival a partial non-refundable deposit is required. If you need to reschedule, we ask for a 24hr heads up. We can normally setup a new date within 2 weeks.
